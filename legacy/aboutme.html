About Me

My name is Aadhav Sivakumar and I'm currently 17 years old and a senior at Dublin High School. I live in Dublin, CA with my mom, dad, and my sister who is currently in college. Since elementary school, I've been interested in the field of engineering, specifically in robotics and software engineering. I participated in FLL (First Lego League), in which you build robots out of lego pieces to compete against others. After that, I started doing VEX, the high school level of robotics. I've been participating for four years, and I was captain of team 5327B in my junior year.

I'm part of my school's enineering academy, and I've taken a couple of engineering courses during high school. This includes Computer Science Principles, Principles of Engineering, AP Computer Science Applications, Digital Electronics. These classes taught me different skills from a variety of different engineering disciplines. In terms of computer science, I learned languages like python, java, SQL, and various other web development languages. As an aspiring engineer, I hope to put my skills to good use and create something that benefits everyone in the future.

I'm cooperative, achievement-oriented, and very talkative. I work in teams well, making sure the goals of the team are put before my personal goals. I'm also very competitive, which drives me to try and do better than those around me. Through this, I learn much from my peers and elders whenever I need help with something. I've learned a lot throughout my engineering experiences and I'm ready to explore the world of technology around me. Thank you for reading, and please take time to explore the rest of the site.
